## **Project Overview**

This project is the final assignment for the **Digital Heritage and Multimedia** course within the
 **DHDK (Digital Humanities and Digital Knowledge)** programme at the University of Bologna.

The game is inspired by the **Museo Internazionale e Biblioteca della Musica di Bologna**, transforming its rich musical heritage, historical documents, and instrument collections into an interactive narrative experience.

Players take on the role of the protagonist, exploring the museum while uncovering story elements, solving puzzles, and gradually unlocking the mysterious sounds heard in a dream.
